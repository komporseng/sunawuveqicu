Helen Johnson-Leipold Net Worth

Helen Johnson-Leipold is an American businesswoman with a net worth of $2.8 billion. She is the great-granddaughter of the founder of SC Johnson and Co and currently runs Johnson Financial and Johnson Outdoors. She graduated from Cornell University and serves as a liaison to the public for SC Johnson and Co. She also chairs the Johnson Foundation at Wingspread, a building designed by Frank Lloyd-Wright. In the past, she briefly chaired her brother’s subsidiary company, Diversey, Inc.

The Johnson family sold the subsidiary after her brother stepped down due to charges of repeated sexual contact with a child. Despite this controversy, Helen Johnson-Leipold has maintained her successful business career and continues to lead Johnson Financial and Johnson Outdoors.
