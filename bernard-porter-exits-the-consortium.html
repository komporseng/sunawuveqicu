Bernard Porter Exits The Consortium

(CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Bernard Porter, one of four partners in Nashville-based artist management and consulting firm the Consortium, has left to pursue other interests. Remaining partners are Mike Martinovich, Stan Moress and Al Schlitz.

Management clients include Joe Diffie, Sherrie Austin, Tammy Cochrane, Mandy Barnett, Catherine Britt and Red Saturday. — Jane Cohen and Bob Grossweiner
