Warriors news: Stephen Curry's wife, Ayesha goes nude for Sweet Body

Ayesha Curry is heating up Instagram after sharing a teaser of her nude photoshoot for her own project Sweet July, a budding brand encompassing a magazine, brick-and-mortar store, and product line. The better half of two-time NBA MVP Stephen Curry boldly flaunted her curves in the tastefully-done photo.

According to Ayesha Curry's caption, the Golden State Warriors superstar even got to pick the image of his choice that will grace the cover of the magazine. Considering the magazine issue will reportedly feature natural wellness and skin remedies, it's only fitting that the radiant Mrs. Curry grace the magazine herself.

“This issue is all about the refresh. It’s about reclaiming your life even in the simplest of ways,” Ayesha wrote in her editor's letter (via Shape.com).

Ayesha added that the election of new US President Joe Biden and Vice President Kamala Harris served as her inspiration for the project.

 “For me, that was the moment that brought about my fresh start and made me realize it was time to recharge, reboot my system, and get ready for all of the beautiful moments, triumphs, and life lessons I hope 2021 brings.”

Led by their basketball superstar patriarch, the Currys have become a fan-favorite family with Ayesha and their three adorable children Riley, Ryan, and Canon. While Stephen Curry is no doubt the most notable among the brood, Ayesha has also paved her own way as a chef, television personality, and author. Apart from being a model family, Steph and Ayesha are also renowned for their philanthropy and generous ways in terms of supporting the community.

Sweet July's spring issue, with Ayesha Curry on the cover, is dropping in newsstands on Friday.
