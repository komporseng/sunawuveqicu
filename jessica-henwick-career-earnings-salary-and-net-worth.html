Jessica Henwick career earnings, salary and net worth

Jessica Henwick was born on August 30, 1992, making her 30 years old as of 2022. She was raised in Surrey, England, a county in the United Kingdom.

She is of British and English descent and practices Christianity.

Henwick played Colleen Wing in the 2017 season of the Netflix series Iron Fist. Although Iron Fist earned mostly unfavorable reviews, Henwick’s portrayal in the series received positive reviews. Both the second season of Luke Cage and the television show The Defenders featured her reprising the part.

At the end of 2017, Henwick was ranked as one of Variety’s Top Breakout Stars. She co-starred in the upcoming films Love and Monsters, On the Rocks, Underwater from Fox, and On the Rocks from Sofia Coppola.

The same year, she was named one of The Hollywood Reporter’s Rising Stars and won the Brit to Watch prize at the Newport Beach Film Festival. In 2020, she received a part in The Gray Man. In 2021, she received a part in Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery.
