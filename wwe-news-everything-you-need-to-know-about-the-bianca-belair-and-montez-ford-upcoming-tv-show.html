Love &amp; WWE: Bianca and Montez : Everything you need to know about the WWE couples upcoming

The Stamford-based promotion is always on the lookout to transcend its existing fanbase and expand its reach among pop culture fans. WWE has previously launched various reality television shows in order to attain this goal. Some of the shows have been immensely popular. This time, WWE introduces a brand-new reality TV program starring Bianca Belair and her husband, Montez Ford.

WWE has officially confirmed that the married couple of Belair and Ford are coming up with a reality-based television show titled “Love & WWE: Bianca and Montez.” The program is scheduled to premiere on February 2, 2024. It will be exclusively streamed on Hulu streaming services. WWE’s official X handle and various other media outlets announced the news.

Additionally, fans will get a sneak peek into Belair and Ford’s personal and professional life in the upcoming reality show. From Belair’s breakthrough to Ford’s ascent to the top, the show will take the viewers through the power couple’s road to WrestleMania.

The program was first announced back in November 2022, and it took quite a while for the couple to finish the show’s filming. Furthermore, it is unclear whether Hulu drops all the episodes simultaneously or the reality show will air on weekly installments. Nonetheless, the WWE Universe now has quite a good reason to subscribe to Hulu.

Bianca Belair opens up about doing a reality show with hubby Montez Ford

As mentioned earlier, WWE is looking to diversify its reality television repertoire with the announcement of a brand-new reality show. In association with the Hulu streaming platform, WWE announced the debut of “Love & WWE: Bianca and Montez.” The eponymous show is an exclusive reality show focusing on the ins and outs of the lives of the power couple, Bianca Belair and Montez Ford.

Prior to the big announcement, Bianca Belair appeared in an interview with Comicbook. In the interview via Fightful, she shared her sentiments about doing a reality show with Ford. The 34-year-old expressed her excitement about the prospect of giving a glimpse of their lives outside the squared circle. However, she also admitted to being slightly nervous.

I will say that I used to have a little bit of downtime at home, but now I guess I won’t have any. The icing on the cake is that I get to do it with my husband, it’s exciting. I am nervous, I never thought that I’d be doing a reality type show but my husband is full of life and when I tell people that the person y’all see in the ring is the person I get at home. So now, I get to share that with the world. But I’m excited and I’m also nervous. We’re exposing ourselves and our family and pulling the curtain back a little bit and showing a balance between who we are in the ring but also outside of the ring. It’s exciting, it is exciting times.
Bianca Belair on Comicbook

Furthermore, Belair stated that she never expected to do a reality show. However, she was thrilled to reveal that Ford is a vivacious person in real life. Thus, she looks forward to unveiling this aspect of his personality to the world. Bianca Belair and Montez Ford announced their engagement back in June 2017, and a year later the couple were married.
