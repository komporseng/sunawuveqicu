Biography

Miguel de Miguel was born in Malaga, Spain on April 17, 1975. An actor who is famous for his career in Spanish-language telenovelas, including on the Colombian series The White Slave in 2016. He rose to fame with his role as Javier González Salmerón in 2005’s Obsesion, as well as Cesar Villa in 2008-2009 on Yo soy Bea. He and Nerea Camacho starred alongside one another on the telenovela The White Slave in 2016. He earned his first on-screen acting credit in 2002 as a guest star on the popular Spanish TV series Hospital Central.
