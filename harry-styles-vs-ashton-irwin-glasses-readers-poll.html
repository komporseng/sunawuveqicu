Harry Styles vs. Ashton Irwin: Who Rocks Glasses Better?

5 Seconds of Summer's Ashton Irwin has been rocking round, thick-rimmed glasses recently, and it totally reminds us of Harry Styles' 'Best Song Ever' alter-ego, Marcel. Who rocks the eyewear best?

While Harry Styles (ahem, Marcel) was supposed to look super nerdy in the 'Best Song Ever' vid, we can't help but still find him totally endearing and still irresistible -- and the glasses just add to that! Harry's entire look is pretty dapper, and we love how his specs give him that intelligent, bookworm look.

Ashton Irwin went with tortoise shell-colored rims, which happen to be one of our favorites. We love how the 5SOS drummer isn't afraid to try new and different styles, and we think the glasses give him that nerdy-punk edge. And we love how they totally compliment those copper-colored curls.
