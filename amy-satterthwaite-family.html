Amy Satterthwaite family, height, current teams, salary, net worth, Instagram

Amy Satterthwaite is a New Zealand cricketer who usually plays and serves as the current vice-captain of New Zealand’s women’s team. She is 35 years of age, born on October 7, 1986, and her place of birth was Christchurch, Canterbury, New Zealand. Amy Satterthwaite entered into domestic cricketing at the age of 17.

Satterthwaite at the moment plays for the Canterbury Magicians in New Zealand at the domestic cricket level. She also plays for the Melbourne Renegades in the Australian Women’s Big Bash League.

Internationally, She has played for New Zealand in Women’s One Day Internationals (WODI) and again since 2007, represented New Zealand on the Women’s Twenty20 Internationals (WT20I) since 2007.

On February 26, 2017, playing against Australia, Amy Satterthwaite became the first player in WODI and second overall following Kumar Sangakkara to score four consecutive hundreds.

In 2017, she was awarded the inaugural ICC Women’s ODI Player of the Year.
