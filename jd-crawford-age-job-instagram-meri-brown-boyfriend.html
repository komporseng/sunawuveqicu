Who Is JD Crawford? Meet Meri Brown Boyfriend! His Age, Job

JD Crawford is the mystery man who was spotted with Sister wives star Meri Brown. This social media appearance ignited budding romance rumors. So who is JD? How old is he? Is JD really Meri’s boyfriend? What job does he do?

All the answers are covered below so find the answers as you keep scrolling down.

Meet JD Crawford, Sister Wives Star Meri Brown Boyfriend

Sister Wives star Meri Brown recently shared a selfie with a mystery man now known as JD Crawford many believed was her new boyfriend. The 52-year-old virtually beamed as she threw her arms around the hunky man, subsequently identified as Hawaii’s “JD Crawford”. Meri thanked him for a pleasant time despite not going into detail about his visit.

“One of my favorite moments from the weekend was hanging out with one of my favorite people!” she captioned the post. “This guy is kind, genuine, and authentic, a true friend. If you don’t have one of these humans in your life, one that has your back, one that no matter what time passes, it’s like no time passed at all, you should get yourself one!

JD flattered by the comment replied, “LOL, Meri … you totally made this trip killer fun! We escaped the escape room, something I hadn’t been able to do by myself! Can’t wait til you come to Hawaii again – I got us some plans! Let’s go!!!!!!!!”

Meri has not made it public that she is in a relationship. However, since she shared a selfie of herself with JD on Instagram, there have been assumptions that she and JD are dating.

JD commented on Meri’s post, making a joke by saying, “Also, do your followers know how boring I am?! hahaha!”

A fan commented, “Please tell me you’re more than just friends. He’s hot! You deserve such happiness in your life.”

Boyfriend? I hope so,” another fan added. “So nice looking together.”

However, we have to surprise you by stating that, JD is gay.

JD is married to his husband Philip Amer Kelley-Crawford. According to Philip’s Facebook, he and JD have been married since 2 June 2013. They reportedly got engaged in August 2010. Philip added, “Well, there you have it folks! A day that can’t be forgotten. Got KEYS to the house, which makes it official! The better half and I are HOMEOWNERS! On top of that, on a very surprising day, he popped the question! I am officially “ENGAGED”! I am the luckiest man alive!!!”

More about JD’s husband, Philip attended Radford High School, Angeles University Foundation, and Leeward Community College. He works as an Area Manager for ESCO, Eurpac Inc.

On their recent anniversary, Phil posted on his IG, “10 years ago today I became a husband to my favorite person in the world. 10 years of love and laughter, and everything in between. Thank you for picking me and for helping me shape to be the best human being. Mahal na mahal kita!”

So, we conclude Meri and JD are just great friends.

JD Crawford Age

Born in October 1975, JD Crawford is 47 years of age.

JD Crawford Job

JD Crawford has a job as a Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and founder at Destination Dance Hawaii. He started the business in 2021. He runs the studio with his husband Philip.

Their website reads, “Introducing Hawaii’s freshest performing arts studio, offering a diverse range of dance, vocal, and musical theatre disciplines. Our primary focus lies in nurturing our students and empowering them to explore their full creative potential with dedication and discipline. At our studio, we prioritize technique-based training and take great pride in our exceptional teaching methods. Inclusivity is at the heart of what we do, as we warmly welcome students from all backgrounds. Our commitment to open and honest communication, absence of contracts, and transparency distinguishes us from other studios across Hawaii.”

According to the website, Philip works as a jazz director meanwhile JD has a role as a Vocal Coach | Glee Club.

They offer several classes to teach different forms and some of them are ballet, tap, hip-hop, contemporary, and ballroom among others. Learn more about the classes they offer via this link here.

Before starting his dance studio, JD worked as an Information Technician at The United States Navy. He served in the Navy from July 1995 to August 2021.

JD graduated from Gardner Edgerton High School in 1994. He studied Human Psychology at the University of Hawaii – West Oahu, Master of Business Administration at the University of Phoenix, and Bachelor of Science in Business and Management at the University of Phoenix.
