Chrishell Stause &amp; Justin Hartley's Divorce Has Officially Been Finalized

The divorce between Chrishell Stause and Justin Hartley has been finalized.

According to reports via US Weekly, the former couple “entered into a confidential settlement as to terms of the divorce, including spousal support.”

Chrishell and Justin went through a very public split in 2019 just after celebrating their second anniversary together.

In an episode of Selling Sunset, Chrishell detailed how Justin informed her of his intention to divorce her.

“I found out because he text[ed] me that we were filed,” she recalled. “…Forty-five minutes later, the world knew.”

Now, both Chrishell and Justin have moved on in their love lives.

It was revealed after her run on Dancing With The Stars that she was dating one of the pro dancers, while Justin is now Instagram official with one of his former co-stars.
